About Us Page

First of all, we want to be called as Team Thunderbolts

By day, they are pixel perfect coders tweaking CSS and writing HTML files, but by night, they're The Thunderbolts, a team of superheros dedicated to fighting bugs, bad UX across the web and keep every project version-controlled! Armed with HTML shields, CSS capes and trusty commits , these heroes wear hoodies, wield VS code and make the web a better place -one merge at a time.

Let's get to know our heroes!

Hi I am Vlad!

I'm from Russia and have been living in Sweden for 8 years. Back home, I danced at a high sports level and even earned the title of Candidate Master of Sports, but after moving here, I completely stopped since I didn't find a place to continue or coach. I spent 5 years studying Game Design at university in Sweden because I love creating games—especially as a gamer myself. In my free time, I sometimes hit the gym, binge series and movies, or dive into Dota (10k+ hours… no big deal, right?). I also enjoy hanging out with friends, whether it's at a cozy bar or just relaxing at home together.

Why do you like frontend development?

Well, since school I always dreamed of being a programmer. Later I realized I also love creating things that people can see and interact with — kind of like how I used to make immersive game worlds. Frontend feels like a cool mix of both: I can still write code, but also make stuff that looks nice and has a bit of atmosphere.

If you have a special power, what would it be?

Uhh...teleport, i guess?
